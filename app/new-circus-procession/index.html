A charming digitally restored vintage circus illustration with modern interactive features. Includes professionally narrated read-along, fun sound effects, and more. Entertaining for adults and readers of all ages.

Background

Originally printed in 1888 by McLoughlin Brothers of New York, this interactive book takes us back to the days when circuses paraded through towns before setting up their Big Top in preparation for the show. The rhythmic stanzas establish the beat of a procession and include all of the acts you’d expect to see in a circus of the era. The original vintage style illustrations were digitally restored and then combined with modern features that both entertain and promote reading, making the experience appealing to readers of all ages.

Features

Features that help promote and reinforce reading skills in younger children:

  • Words are highlighted in narration mode as the stanzas are read aloud.
  • Words can be touched individually to hear only that word read aloud.
  • Words and sounds appear with most illustration touch points.
  • Fun sound effects are hidden throughout the illustrations.

More Details
