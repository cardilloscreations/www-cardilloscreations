eyeZ

A silly pair of droid googly eyes that roll around as you move the device. Nothing super amazing... just some silly fun.

FREE on Google Play

Enjoy this silly pair of droid googly eyes that roll around as you move the device. It’s not going to change your life so don’t expect too much. Just a silly pair of eyes. Those familiar with Xeyes on X-Windows may also appreciate this app.

Features

  • Realistic fluid based movement using accelerometer feedback and advanced physics.
  • Options to make eyes either “fixed” or “floating” based on your preference.
